# 📊 Supply Chain Analytics Dashboard

## Overview
This repository contains a **3‑page interactive Business Intelligence dashboard** built to analyze and optimize supply chain performance.  
The dashboards provide insights into **financials, logistics efficiency, supplier performance, and inventory intelligence**, enabling data‑driven decision making.

---

## 1. Executive Supply Chain Overview
![Executive Dashboard](https://github.com/Amit-DataDoc23/Supply-Chain-Analytics-Dashboard/blob/fd125d68c254b705c966786783aad3540aa1d0da/Executive%20Overview.png)

### Key Highlights
- **Revenue:** $605.6K | **Gross Profit:** $208.3K | **GP%:** 34.40%  
- **OTIF%:** 92.55% | **Late Deliveries:** 164 | **Stockout Orders:** 75  
- **Return Qty Units:** 460 | **Waste Qty Units:** 606  

### Trends & Insights
- **Revenue & Profit Trend:** Monthly revenue fluctuates between $39K–$58K, with gross profit consistently around $14K–$20K.  
- **OTIF Performance:** Generally stable above 90%, with dips in May–August indicating delivery challenges.  
- **Channel Contribution:** Retail dominates ($0.25M), followed by HoReCa, Distributor, and E‑commerce.  
- **Customer Analysis:** Scatter plot shows most customers clustered around **GP% 30–35%** and **OTIF 90–95%**, highlighting reliable profitability with moderate delivery consistency.

## 2. Operations & Logistics Performance
![Operations Dashboard](https://github.com/Amit-DataDoc23/Supply-Chain-Analytics-Dashboard/blob/fd125d68c254b705c966786783aad3540aa1d0da/Operation%20%26%20Logistics.png)

### Key Highlights
- **OTIF%:** 92.55% | **Stockout Orders:** 75 | **Quality Issues:** 61 | **Avg Delay Days:** 1.95  

### Trends & Insights
- **Stockouts by Category:** Beverages (29) and Snacks (26) are the most affected, signaling demand‑supply imbalance.  
- **Late Deliveries by Warehouse:** W005 shows the highest average delay (2.4 days).  
- **Supplier Scorecard:**  
  - S004 leads with **98.39% OTIF** and lowest delays.  
  - S008 has highest revenue ($112K) but poor OTIF (85.29%) and most quality issues (19).  
- **Regional Late Deliveries:** East Europe accounts for **73.78% of delays**, a major operational bottleneck.  
- **Quality Issues:** Concentrated in Dairy (S003), Frozen (S005), and Snacks (S008), pointing to supplier‑specific risks.

## 3. Root Cause & Inventory Intelligence
![Root Cause Dashboard](https://github.com/Amit-DataDoc23/Supply-Chain-Analytics-Dashboard/blob/fd125d68c254b705c966786783aad3540aa1d0da/Risk%20Cause%20%26%20Inventory%20Intelligence.png)

### Key Highlights
- **Closing Stock Units:** 2M | **Expired Qty Units:** 8K  
- **Returns:** 460 units | **Waste:** 606 units  

### Trends & Insights
- **Inventory Health:** Scatter plot shows categories with high closing stock also facing higher expiry risks.  
- **Returns by Category:** Beverages (153) and Snacks (138) dominate returns, suggesting product quality or demand mismatch.  
- **Risk Inventory by Warehouse:** W004 and W001 lead with ~710 units at risk, requiring tighter controls.  
- **Waste Distribution:** Bakery (302) and Dairy (207) contribute most to waste, highlighting perishable product challenges.  
- **Profitability Drivers:**  
  - HoReCa channel and Beverages category linked to Supplier S008 show strong gross profit contribution.  
  - Retail remains the most profitable channel ($83.7K), followed by HoReCa and Distributor.
